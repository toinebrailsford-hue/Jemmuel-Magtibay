My career

About

Jem is a professional musician and producer from Sydney, Australia. He has worked across live shows, studio sessions, albums, touring, church events, music direction, and content creation for music-focused brands.

His approach is warm, energetic, and song-first: serve the idea, build the tone, and bring the feeling all the way through.
